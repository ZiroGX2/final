Welcome

 

This site contains hashes for clean rom dumps of certain games.

You can use this site to verify your roms validity for netplay, lunar magic, net64, and for use with other tools.

 

Tools

 

This site also has the location of many tools that are related to the games that have hashes stored on the site.

There is also an md5 checker in the Tools section to verify your hashes.
